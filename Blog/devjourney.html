A developer Journey

Posted by Carl Hamilton on August 24, 2017

Over the last year I was working in a field that my interest for was dwindling, I have always loved I.T and technology but things had to change. After a discussion with my wife we decided that it would be best if I took my love for technology back into coding. I had originally started off my career taking up development with C++ but I wasn't able to continue due to health at the time.

As my journey began I had to decide what technologies I was going to learn and so my adventure started.

So I first started writing down a series of technologies to learn such as:

  • HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap
  • Angular, Node.JS,React, PHP

I then thought I should learn Microsoft technologies such as:

  • C# and .NET
  • ASP.NET, MVC and Winforms

I have seen many Youtube videos state that it is best to specialise, however I feel my learning experience is much better to get a foundation with multiple languages.
