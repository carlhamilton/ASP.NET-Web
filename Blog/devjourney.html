Why I started this new adventure

Posted by Carl Hamilton on September 6th, 2017

Over the last year I have been working in a field that my interest for was dwindling, I have always loved IT and technology but things had to change. After a discussion with my wife we decided that it would be best if I took my love for technology back into coding.

I had originally started off my career taking up development with C++ but I wasn't able to continue due to family issues at the time.

My journey began for two reasons, the first was how amazing it is to build new things and making use of new technologies and to take on new projects. The second was my daughter, Sienna Hamilton who was born on the 15th May 2016 and that day my life changed forever.

As my journey began I had to decide what technologies I was going to learn and so my adventure started.

So I first started writing down a series of technologies to learn such as:

  • HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap
  • Angular, Node.JS,React, PHP

I then thought I should learn Microsoft technologies such as:

  • C# and .NET
  • ASP.NET, MVC, Winforms and WPF
  • I will be looking at further developing my SQL skills using both MySQL and Microsoft SQL

I have seen many Youtube videos state that it is best to specialise, however I feel my learning experience is much better to get a foundation with multiple languages.

What resources should be used?

This is a great question and one I certainly had when I was starting out, and one we are going to answer with a series of blog posts.

First off I will list a few of the resources I use:

  • PluralSight
  • Stackoverflow
  • Freecodecamp
  • Youtube
  • Podcasts for me such as DotNet Rocks and Complete Developer Podcast

Youtube as a resource can be overwhelming, who should you follow? Is the technology up to date? Questions like this are expected but keep searching there is so much useful information to learn on Youtube.

In future Blog posts I will link to some great Youtube resources :-)
